# LV-12V-100Ah FAQ

## General Questions

### Q: How long does the battery last?
A: 6000 cycles, approx. 10 years under normal use.

### Q: Can it be installed outdoors?
A: No, IP20 rating requires indoor installation.

### Q: Can old and new batteries be mixed?
A: No, must use same model and batch.

## Technical Questions

### Q: How many units can be connected in parallel?
A: Max 4 units, total capacity 400Ah.

### Q: What solar panel size is recommended?
A: Solar panel power should be 1.5-2x battery capacity.

### Q: Can battery charge and discharge simultaneously?
A: Yes, supports pass-through charging.

## Installation Questions

### Q: Is professional installation required?
A: Yes, have a licensed electrician perform installation.

### Q: Can I wire it myself?
A: Yes, but follow manual instructions and ensure correct breaker size.
