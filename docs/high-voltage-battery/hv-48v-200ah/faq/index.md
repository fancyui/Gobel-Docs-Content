# HV-48V-200Ah FAQ

## General Questions

### Q: What's the battery lifespan?
A: 8000 cycles, approx. 15 years under normal use.

### Q: Can it be installed outdoors?
A: No, must be installed in dedicated battery room.

### Q: How much space is needed?
A: Recommend 4 square meters including maintenance access.

## Technical Questions

### Q: What's the charge/discharge efficiency?
A: Charge efficiency ≥ 98%, discharge efficiency ≥ 95%.

### Q: How many units can be paralleled?
A: Max 10 units, total capacity 96kWh.

### Q: What inverter is needed?
A: Supports 48V off-grid/grid-tie inverters, power ≥ 5kW.

### Q: Can it be monitored remotely?
A: Yes, via cloud platform or mobile app.

## Installation Questions

### Q: How long does installation take?
A: Single unit approx. 2 hours, parallel systems need additional time.

### Q: Is power capacity increase required?
A: According to local policy, grid connection requires utility approval.

### Q: Can I install it myself?
A: No, must be installed by professional team.

## Maintenance Questions

### Q: How often is maintenance required?
A: Professional maintenance annually, monthly basic checks.

### Q: What if battery degrades?
A: Normal degradation. Consider replacement if capacity drops below 80%.

### Q: How to report faults?
A: Call technical support hotline or submit ticket via app.
