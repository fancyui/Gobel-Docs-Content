# LP-21700 User Manual

## Daily Use

### Charging

1. Use a dedicated LiFePO4 charger
2. Charging temperature: 0°C ~ 45°C
3. Charge current should not exceed 1C
4. Disconnect charger after full charge

### Discharging

1. Discharge current should not exceed 2C
2. Discharge temperature: -20°C ~ 60°C
3. Avoid deep discharge (DOD > 80%)

## Maintenance

### Regular Inspection

- Check voltage monthly
- Check appearance monthly
- Check connections quarterly

### Cleaning

- Wipe with dry cloth
- Do not use chemical cleaners

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Possible Cause | Solution |
|-------|-----------------|----------|
| Overheating during charge | Charge current too high | Reduce charge current |
| Fast voltage drop | Capacity degradation | Replace battery |
| Cannot charge | BMS fault | Check protection circuit |

## Disposal

Please recycle batteries according to local regulations. Do not dispose of in regular trash.
